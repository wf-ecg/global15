Submit Photos & Video

Give us a smile!

We are seeking headshots of all G15 attendees for inclusion in the G15 Bios booklet. Please send us your headshot — it can be your professional photo from your corporate bio or a selfie — to GFI@wellsfargo.com. We need all headshots in by December 8, 2014.

Purpose

In preparation for the G15 meeting, we are seeking video and photo submissions from every GFI office for inclusion in Chuck’s opening remarks and throughout the G15 conference materials and presentations.

Request of you

  • Office video: Create a 1-3 minute office video that shows your interpretation of the G15 theme of Winning as a Team: Partner, Trust and Leverage. For example, you can consider performing a skit or a spoof off of a movie, but please note that we are not allowed to use original music or clips from actual movies or TV shows due to copyright issues. Feel free to include GFI team members, Support and Product Partners in this video.
  • Office photo: Take a team photo in front of a famous landmark, icon or building that is specific to your office location or country/state (e.g. the GFI Paris office could take a group photo in front of the Eiffel Tower; the Dallas office could a photo in front of Cowboys Stadium). You may also take photos of your business group (e.g. GFI COR, GFI CSU).

Be creative! Feel free to dress up, use props and have some fun! We are seeking videos and office photos that are both creative and original, and most importantly, that truly exemplify and demonstrate the G15 theme.

Equipment for recording videos

Use any type of recording devices available to you, including iPhones or other mobile devices, cameras, or camcorders.

Note: If recording from mobile devices, please shoot in the landscape format instead of portrait format. Please do not seek the services of a professional in the creation of the video. It should be a reflection of the teamwork from each office.

Video file formats

Preferred file formats are .mp4, .mov, .wmv or .avi. While we would like each video to be 100% completed from a production standpoint, we will have resources available if some additional editing is required.
