Welcome to the Global 15 Microsite.

This year’s theme - “Winning as a Team: Partner, Trust and Leverage” - continues to reinforce our core vision of being an organization where our people are the cornerstone of our business. By working as one team, trusting and leveraging our partners and our team members, we can continue to be ever more successful. During the Global 15 (G15) conference, our aim is to reinforce and build on our one-team spirit, inspire new partnerships, and provide exciting learning opportunities to challenge and inspire you, while deepening your knowledge-base.

On this microsite, you will find the most current, helpful, and relevant information about G15, including our exciting program agenda, various learning and development break-out sessions, our Product Partner Expo, team building activities, and much more.

As we will refresh and augment the content provided here, please bookmark this page and visit often. We have an exciting G15 program lined up and look forward to a week that will surely be an enhancing and enriching developmental experience.
