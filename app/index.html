Welcome to the Global 15 Microsite!

This year’s theme - “Winning as a Team: Partner, Trust and Leverage” - acknowledges the belief that our people remain the cornerstone of our business. Only by working together as a team can we be successful. During the Global 15 (G15) conference, we will aim to build a one-team spirit, inspire new partnerships, and deepen your knowledge-base.

On this microsite, you will find the most current, helpful, and relevant information about G15, including our exciting program agenda, various learning and development break-out sessions, our Product Partner Expo, team building activities, and much more.

As we will refresh and augment the content provided here, please bookmark this page and visit often. We have an exciting G15 program lined up and look forward to a week that will surely be an enhancing and enriching developmental experience.
