Welcome to the Global 15 Microsite!

Here, you will find everything you need to know about the Global 15 (G15) conference. The theme for G15 is “Winning as a Team: Partner, Trust and Leverage” in acknowledgement of the deeply held belief that our people remain the cornerstone of our business and only by working together as a team, can we be successful. We look forward to the opportunity during G15 to build team spirit, inspire new partnerships, and deepen your knowledge-base.

This site has been developed to provide you with the most current, helpful and relevant information about the G15 conference including, our exciting program agenda, the various learning and development break-out sessions that we’ll be holding, our Product Partner Expo, team building activities…and much more!

We will continually refresh and add to the content provided here so please bookmark this page and visit the site regularly to learn the latest information about G15. Once again, we have an exciting G15 program lined up and look forward to a week that will surely be a deeply enhancing and enriching developmental experience.
