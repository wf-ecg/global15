Learning & Development sessions

Learning & Development sessions

During Global 15, you will be offered eight learning and development courses that focus on a variety of topics, including both hard and soft skills, in the area of professional development.

A total of six hours of learning and development sessions will be held: four hours on Monday and two hours on Wednesday. All attending GFI team members will be required to partake in the six sessions. Product partners and other attendees are also welcome to attend.

Attendees will be able to choose their preferred courses. There will be no mandatory sessions during G15. We will provide program descriptions of each of the learning and development sessions shortly, so please visit this space for more information.
