Awards Night

GFI Annual Awards Dinner and Ceremony

Join our glamorous Global 15 awards ceremony on Wednesday, January 28.

We have an exciting evening lined up for you as we recognize and celebrate our colleagues’ accomplishments in 2014. We will also see the Deal of the Year nominations and you will have the opportunity to vote for the winning video. Our wonderful hosts for the evening are Caroline Baudinet-Stumpf and Walter Triches who will be sure to make it a fun filled evening you won’t forget!

Attire for the evening is either your national dress or cocktail attire.
